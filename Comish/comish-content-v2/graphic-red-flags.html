Red Flags
1

"Effective commission rate"

They're hiding the real math. Ask for actual tier rates.

2

Retroactive quota changes

If they can move goalposts mid-quarter, accelerators mean nothing.

3

Paid on "collected" not closed

You close in March, customer pays in June. You might be gone.

4

Clawbacks beyond 90 days

Churn happens. 6-month clawbacks are punishment, not policy.

5

"Up to" language everywhere

"Earn up to 15%" means almost nobody earns 15%.

Your comp plan is a contract. Read it like one.
